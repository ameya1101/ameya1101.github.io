Modelling Neutron Star Parameters using Bayesian Neural Networks

A Study Oriented Project under Dr Kinjal Banerjee, Department of Physics

The Equation of State (EOS) of a neutron star enables us to deduce its structure and behaviour; the EOS is often parameterised by certain key Nuclear Matter Parameters (NMPs) which can be used to form the EOS and subsequently to deduce important properties about the star. In this project, I am attempting to develop a Bayesian neural network-based model to predict neutron star properties such as its mass, radius and tidal deformability using a multivariate Gaussian distribution of the NMPs as the input.

Hybrid Quantum-Classical Computation

A study at Infinity Labs, UST Global, Thiruvanathapuram

We evaluated the performance of a well known quantum machine learning algorithm — the quantum support vector machine — on a dataset and compared it to its classical counterparts as well as quantum annealing approaches. This study was largely a reproduction of the results obtained by Li, R. Y. et al. (2019) in [1].

[1] Li, Richard Y., et al. “Unconventional machine learning of genome-wide human cancer data.” arXiv preprint arXiv:1909.06206 (2019).
