Modelling Neutron Star Properties using Artificial Neural Networks Supervisor: Dr. Kinjal Banerjee
The Equation of State (EOS) of a neutron star is parameterised by certain key Nuclear Matter Parameters (NMPs) which help to deduce important properties about the star. We attempt to develop a neural network-based model to predict neutron star properties such as its mass, radius and tidal deformability using a multivariate Gaussian distribution of the NMPs as the input. Using rhe model, we also unveil correlations between nuclear matter parameters and neutron star properties.
SugarGain - International Genetically Engineered Machines 2020 Supervisor: Dr. Sumit Biswas [ Wiki ] [ PyPI ]

Participated in the iGEM 2020 competition where we developed a solution to reducing post-harvest sucrose losses in sugarcane using genetically engineered biological systems. I primarily worked on the mathematical modelling and software teams. Our work is documented on the project wiki and the software we developed is available as a python package on PyPI.
Hybrid Quantum-Classical Computation Supervisor: Simsarul Vengasseri, UST Global
We evaluated the performance of a well known quantum machine learning algorithm — the quantum support vector machine — on an oncological dataset and compared it to its classical counterparts as well as quantum annealing approaches. This study was largely a reproduction of the results obtained by Li, R. Y. et al. (2019).
Neutrino Oscillations and Associated Symmetries Supervisor: Dr. Radhika Vathsan [ Paper ]

A term paper on the origins of neutrino oscillations and the symmetries associated with this phenomenon, such as CPT invariance and CP violation. This paper was written as a part of the capstone project for the course Quantum Mechanics II (PHY F311).
